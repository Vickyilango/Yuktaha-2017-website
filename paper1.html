Innovia

   “There is not a lot of forms where you can control your presentations and your ideas”
Courage is what it takes to stand up and speak and to sit down and listen. So here we provide you a platform to express your thoughts and to develop your own technical skills. Come and excel in both presenting and technical skills.


DOMAIN
Your topic can be any of the below specified ones or any other topics regarding Computer Science and Information Technology are invited.
• IOT
• Cloud Computing
• Grid Computing
• Distributed systems
• Data mining and warehousing
• Artificial Intelligence
• Blue Brain
• 5g

RULES
• The paper should be submitted in IEEE Format
• Each team may have maximum of 3 members
• The name of the file should be the title of the paper
• Selected team will be intimated through mail
• Selected team should carry both the soft copy and the hard copy of the paper.
• Selected teams must present their paper in MS Powerpoint
• Presentation should not be more than 10 mins including the query session.(7 mins presentation + 3 mins of query session)
• Judges decision is final
• The intimation of the selected abstracts will be sent on 15th February
• Your paper should be of minimum 3 and maximum of 5 pages

The paper should be submitted on or before 13.02.2017 (before 11.50 pm)

Energeia

“ENERGEIA” event provides an ideal platform for the thinkers of this generation. For those who wish to voice their innovative ideas in an effective manner, we provide an opportunity to network, compete and grow with the best.

DOMAIN
The presentation of paper is invited in the following areas
• Bio-energy
• Smart grid
• Carbon capture and storage
• Smart meters
• Micro grid
• Smart and sustainable cities
• Solar (photo voltaic)

RULES
• The number of participants in a team is 2
• Please bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen drive (or CD) on the day of the event
• Please bring 2 hardcopies of the submitted paper on the day of event
• Each team will get 10 minutes for the presentation followed by question and answer session
• Last date to submit your soft copy of abstract is 13th February 2017.If you are shortlisted, you will be notified on 14th Feb.
• Please include your name, e-mail id and mobile number in the abstract
• Your paper should be in .docx format
• Please mail your abstract, and presentation to the mail id given below.The decision of the judges is final
